# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want TradingView.Screener to be a welcoming project for users and contributors. Be respectful, assume good intent, and keep discussions focused on improving the software.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and receive constructive feedback professionally.
- Focus issue and pull request discussion on the technical topic at hand.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic comments after a maintainer asks to refocus.
- Any conduct that makes participation unsafe or unwelcoming.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate, edit, or remove comments and may restrict participation when needed to keep the project healthy. To report a concern, contact the maintainer through the GitHub profile linked from this repository.
